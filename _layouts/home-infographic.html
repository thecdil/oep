Description

This portal has been developed by the library faculty and staff at the University of Idaho. It hosts digital content curated to be of interest and use to K-12 educators in the state of Idaho.

The portal contains six inquiry kits: 1910 Fires, Idaho Women's History Japanese American Experiences of Internment, Logging in North Idaho, Regional Oral Histories of Idaho, and Idaho Connections to National Events which link to curated primary sources from digital collections.
