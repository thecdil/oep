# Environmental history inquiry kit

## What is this?

This is a theme-based inquiry kit, based on our [inspiration from Maryland](https://thinkport.org/tps/indigenouskits/inquiry-kits.html?unit=1&topic=1). The two layouts are different options for how the items could look on the page. The full page could include items around a theme (e.g. environmantal history), plus interpretive content and explanations for educators.
