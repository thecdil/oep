# Browsing Collections 

This page is a handy index of the 20+ collections that have materials featured on this site. Each collection description includes basic information about the collection, called metadata in library lingo, as well as a brief explanation as to why one of our librarians thought the collection might be of interest to K-12 teachers.

## Reference Shelf

In addition to collections, we hope to create an index of some useful ebooks and other digital texts.

![Idaho Histories cover](/objects/IdahoHistories.png)[Idaho Histories](https://uidaho.pressbooks.pub/idahohistories) is an original ebook created at the University of Idaho in 2023. It is a short, digital ebook that uses the Library of Congress method on inquiry described on our [how to guide](https://thecdil.github.io/oep/howto.html) to encourage students to explore primary archival objects.
