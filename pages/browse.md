# Browsing Collections 

This page is a handy index of the 20+ collections that have selected records featured in our inquiry kits. Each collection description includes basic information about the collection, called metadata in library lingo, as well as a brief explanation as to why one of our librarians thought the collection might be of interest to K-12 teachers.

<br>

## Reference Shelf

In addition to featured collections, we plan to create a digital reference shelf of some useful ebooks and other digital texts as they're added to this site.


{% include feature/image.html objectid="/objects/IdahoHistories.png" width="25" display="inline" link="https://uidaho.pressbooks.pub/idahohistories" %}

[Idaho Histories](https://uidaho.pressbooks.pub/idahohistories) is an original ebook created at the University of Idaho in 2023. It is a short, digital ebook that uses the Library of Congress method on inquiry described on our [how to guide](https://thecdil.github.io/oep/howto.html) to encourage students to explore primary archival objects.
